Detention Room

Detention Room is a First-Person Multiplayer Shooter. This Game is an objective-based shooter. There are two teams the students and the teachers. The students need to leave school while the teachers try to stop them. The weapons are school-based items. They can attack with chalk, rulers, their hands, sponges, and many more school-themed weapons.

This game is still in development. The game is being developed in Unity by a team of 4.
