Detention Room

Multiplayer FPS

Detention Room is a First Person Multiplayer Shooter. This Game is a objective based shooter. There are two teams the students and the teachers. The students need to leave the school while the teachers try to stop them. The weapons are school based items. They can attack with chalk, rulers, their hands, sponges and many more school themed weapons.

This game is still in development. The game is being developed in Unity by a team of 4.

Hungry Rivers

Cooperative Tower-Builder

Hungry Rivers was a group project were we had to buil an arcade. As we wanted a cooperative game we decided for a table top arcade. The game has a two and four player mode in which players have to build up their food towers while not losing their food items. Each player has his own ability which becomes usfull in tricky situation so players are incentivised to comunicate if they need a specific effect to help themselfs.

The game was done in unity but has still a couple of bugs as we rushed to finish it on time.

CULTH's Bane

Dungeon Crawler

CULTH's Bane was inspired by "Slay the Spire", in which the player has to fight his way through randomly generated rooms. The difference is that the player can only play one card per turn. At the end of the turn, his whole hand is discarded. As we only had 2 days, we didn't manage to finish the game, so it's a buggy and lacks feedback.
The game was developed in Unity.

Shelter

Shelter was done during the GlobalGameJam2019, where we decide to do a 2D platformer. We hit a lot of obstacles as it was our first Game Jam but we got something which is playable. In Shelter you control a man who finds himself in the woods during winter and needs to find/repair his shelter. So he adventures out to find items like a torch to keep himself warm.
