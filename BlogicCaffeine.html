BLogic Caffeine

Blogic is a 3D puzzle game that I developed in a team of 5 developers for a 5th semester university project. In this sugar-themed cuboid puzzle game, the player controls a sugar cube that can be moved on a 3-dimensional grid, through an environment created out of chocolate pieces, filled with all sorts of dangers, like coffee drops that dissolve you on touch. The player is not an ordinary sugar cube though. He has the ability to attach other brown-sugar-cubes to him and move with them. This allows the player to reach higher places on the grid, overcome gaps, open up or block passages and complete levels in the quest to get him to safety. While navigating through the chocolate filled world, the player is constantly “chased” by the slowly rising coffee level, which threatens to dissolve him. The player’s goal is to reach the safe sugar box at the end of the level, before he gets drowned by the coffee.

Blogic was developed using the MonoGame framework.
